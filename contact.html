Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping within the US typically takes 5-7 business days. International shipping can take 10-14 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout.

Do you offer returns or exchanges?

Yes, we accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be in their original condition. Custom or personalized items cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective.

Are your products handmade?

Yes, all our products are handcrafted by skilled artisans. Each piece is made with care and may have slight variations that showcase its handmade nature.

Can I request a custom order?

Absolutely! We love creating custom pieces. Please contact us with your ideas, and we'll work with our artisans to bring your vision to life.

How do I care for my handmade items?

Care instructions vary by product type and materials. Each item comes with specific care instructions, but generally, we recommend gentle handling and avoiding exposure to harsh chemicals.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location and will be calculated at checkout.
