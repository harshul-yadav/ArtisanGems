Our Story

Celebrating artisanal craftsmanship and sustainable handmade goods

How It All Started

ArtisanGems was born from a passion for authentic craftsmanship and a desire to support traditional artisans in a world increasingly dominated by mass production.

Our founder, Aisha, traveled across different regions of India and was captivated by the incredible skills of local artisans who created beautiful pieces using techniques passed down through generations. Seeing how many of these artisans struggled to reach a wider market inspired her to create a platform that would connect these talented creators with appreciative customers nationwide and globally.

Since our humble beginnings in 2015, we've grown into a community of over 50 artisans from across India, each bringing their unique cultural heritage and skills to our carefully curated collection.

Our Values

Authentic Craftsmanship

We celebrate and promote traditional techniques and handcrafted excellence, ensuring every piece tells a story of skill and dedication.

Sustainability

We prioritize eco-friendly materials and sustainable practices, minimizing our environmental footprint while creating beautiful products.

Ethical Partnerships

We ensure fair compensation and supportive relationships with all our artisans, honoring their skills and supporting their communities.

Unique Expression

We believe in the beauty of individuality, offering pieces that help customers express their personal style with meaningful, one-of-a-kind items.

Meet Our Artisans

The talented hands and creative minds behind our unique collections

Priya

Jewelry Artisan

With over 20 years of experience, Priya creates delicate jewelry pieces using traditional metalworking techniques passed down through generations in her family from Jaipur.

Vikram

Textile Artisan

Vikram's family has been creating hand-woven textiles for generations. His intricate patterns combine traditional Indian techniques with contemporary designs that reflect his heritage from Gujarat.

Ananya

Ceramic Artist

Ananya's distinctive ceramic pieces blend traditional aesthetics with functional design, each handcrafted in her studio using locally-sourced clay and techniques from her ancestors in Khurja.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Eco-Friendly Materials

We prioritize sustainable, recycled, and upcycled materials wherever possible, reducing waste and environmental impact.

Minimal Packaging

Our packaging is plastic-free, made from recycled materials, and designed to be reusable or biodegradable.

Environmental Initiatives

For every purchase, we contribute to reforestation projects and other environmental conservation efforts.

Carbon-Offset Shipping

We partner with carbon-neutral shipping providers and offset any remaining emissions from delivery.
